My Favourite Movies of all time

My Top 3 Movies of all time

Fight Club - 9.5

A film that speaks about reality. An insomniac office worker and a devil-may-care soap maker form an underground fight club that evolves into much more.

Director : David Fincher
Cast : Ed Nortan, Brad Pitt, Meat Loaf
Music : The Dust Brothers

Sicario - 9.0

Most realistic military movie. An idealistic FBI agent is enlisted by a government task force to aid in the escalating war against drugs at the border area between the U.S. and Mexico.

Director : Denis Villisburg
Cast : Benicio Del Toro, Josh Brolin, Emily Blunt
Music : Johann Johannson

Joker - 9.8

A film about mentally ill person. During the 1980s, a failed stand-up comedian is driven insane and turns to a life of crime and chaos in Gotham City while becoming an infamous psychopathic crime figure.

Director : Todd Philips
Cast : Joaquin Phoenix, Robert De Niro
Music : Hilder Guonadottir
